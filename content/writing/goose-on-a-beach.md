It's a lovely morning on the System, and you're a horrible goose. Your
down-tree isn't a horrible goose, but they woke up and chose chaos
today, so here you are.

You are on a beach. People are drawing in the sand. Everything they
draw'll go away overnight, but that's the point. You aren't here to
ponder impermanence, though - you are merely a horrible goose.

You sneak up on a robot. They're etching a random pattern into the sands
to keep their motor skills up.

*HONK!*

The robot jolts forward, flattening their work with their wheels. You
run away. You are a mischievous goose.

A cat-lady sets down her rake. You sprint over. You snatch it. You run
away and hide the rake on the other end of the beach. The cat chases
after you. You don't mind; you are a horrible goose.

You lay low for a moment. You dart in to grab someone's unguarded
sandwich and eat it, horribly.

All this racket wakes up a dog, an unusually dog-shaped one. He looks at
you. He is a person who is a dog, and he knows you are a person who is a
horrible goose. That's even your signifier, #HorribleGoose.

You hear a bark in your head and also from the boardwalk. It's a deep
bark, a "Hey, you!" bark.

You pause to look at the dog. He is Scout By The Washes-Away-Paintings
Beach, and he knows who you are.

\<That's enough, yeah?\> Scout asks.

You honk and send back a shrug. You are a horrible goose.

\<You've made your point. Solid performance art, if that's your deal.
Now can you stop bugging everyone?\>

\<Or?\> you ask.

There are two dogs.

\<I'll chase you away!\> / \<I'll start making calls.\>

The dog runs at you, tail wagging / sits back and tilts its head to one
side.

You puff yourself up, spread your wings, and hiss. You are a menacing
goose, no?

The dog runs at you / ignores you.

You run past the charging dog, aiming for the Scout calling people. The
sim's owners aren't immediately public, but you don't want to get your
fun cut short if he finds them.

The dog turns around to catch you, barking / barely notices your most
fearsome threat display.

You run straight through the dog sitting on the boardwalk. You honk at
him on principle - noclip is rude!

The dog stops in front of you / stands up.

\<Fun chase?\> / \<So *do* I need to bother people with bounce ACLs?\>

This could turn into actual consequences. You don't want actual
consequences. You're just a horrible goose.

You hop sims into a park. You don't see any dogs there. Good. You look
for chaos to cause, for you are a cladist who's a horrible goose.

By the beach, the Scout instances merge down. His tail wags, and he
spins around a few times before lying down for a nap. *That's enough
work for today.*
