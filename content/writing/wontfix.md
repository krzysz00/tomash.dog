"That bad?" the other Tomash asked.

"None of us needed to be there. I know *I* didn't need to be there. Sure, it *sounded* like some sort of bug around self-referential exits, that'd gotten ignored, hence the meeting, but no. "Hundredth and a half, I think ... I ducked out near what was probably the end to help someone who got stuck in zero-G."

The other Tomash stood up. "Glad I didn't get sucked into that argument."

"Yeah, I'm glad I forked for this one. Now, would you mind merging down? I could use less irritating memories for the last half hour."
